Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 04, 2026

At Shabd, we believe that your privacy is just as important as your education. This policy outlines our commitment to being transparent about how we (don't) handle your data.

"Our philosophy is simple: Your data belongs on your device, not our servers."

1. Information Collection

Shabd is designed to function entirely offline and locally. We do not collect, store, or transmit any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location.

2. Data Usage

Since no data is collected, we have no access to your usage patterns. We do not use tracking cookies, we do not serve personalized advertisements, and we do not sell data to third-party brokers.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not utilize any third-party SDKs (like Facebook or Google Analytics) that could track your behavior across other apps or websites.

4. Children’s Privacy

Shabd is a safe environment for all ages. Because we do not collect any information, we are fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this document to reflect new features. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Effective Date."
