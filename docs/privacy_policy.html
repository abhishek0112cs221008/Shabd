Privacy Policy

Welcome to Shabd. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

Shabd is designed to be an offline-first, privacy-respecting application. We believe in minimal data collection:

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your usage behavior or navigation within the app.
  • Local Storage: All your progress, favorite words, and history are stored locally on your device only.

2. Notifications

Shabd uses local notifications to deliver your Daily Word. These notifications are scheduled and triggered entirely within your device. No external servers are involved in sending these periodic reminders.

3. Third-Party Services

To keep the app free and functional, we may use certain third-party services that could collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for core app functionality on Android.
  • Indus App Store Services: Used for distribution and updates.

4. Data Security

Since your data lives on your device, its security is as strong as your device's security. We recommend keeping your operating system updated and using device-level encryption.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
